About Ghandour Ushering Agency

Adding A Touch Of Glamour.

Ghandour Ushering Agency is a private owned agency that is set up to professionally assist event planners and vendors during events. Ghandour Ushering offers protocol sevices such as ushering, dowry carrying, product activations, guest check-in list, commercials and video vixening.

Our unique style of uniformity has been well recommended by our clients such as 3 Music Media Networks, Decorsign Ghana, Hubtel Ghana, Fancy Events, Eagle Eye Events, KFK Events, Artpreciate Awards, and many more.

Ghandour ushering Agency is fast becoming one of Ghana's most trusted brands when it comes to event ushering in general due to our unique way of delivering high standard services. New recruited hostesses are taken through online and in-house training and presentations to give them insight and groom them into top professionals.

Ghandour ushering Agency has a set standard of offering the best deals during bookings with the clients paying 70% during booking and 30% after service.

Meet The CEO

George Acolatse Junior

George Acolatse Junior is the Founder and CEO of Ghandour Ushering Agency. The Ghandour Ushering Project started off due to his love for events and more importantly, serving humanity. He came up with the name Ghandour which is an Arab word meaning Richies or Posperity.

His love for events compelled him to recruit young passionate ladies aspiring to be event hostesses and trained them to deliver services with high level standards. George also known as Malvin, a name by his god father, has vast experience in Human Resource Management due to his educational and professional background.

George is principled young man and with his traits and vision, has attained a prominent status that has set him apart from the rest. His love for grooming young aspiring persons in the event industry is unmatched.
